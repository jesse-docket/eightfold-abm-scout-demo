Why HCA Healthcare

Built for high-volume clinical hiring under a workforce shortage.

HCA operates one of the largest care networks in the US — around 190 hospitals plus roughly 124 ambulatory surgery centers — and recruited more than 116,500 colleagues, including 37,500 bedside nurses, in a single year. These are the pressures Eightfold is designed to relieve.

A deepening clinical shortage

Nursing shortages are projected across 42 of 50 states by 2030, alongside an estimated shortfall of about 86,000 physicians by 2036. Demand for clinicians is structurally outpacing supply.

Turnover and a retention crisis

Registered-nurse turnover ran around 16% in 2024, more than 40% of nurses considered leaving in the past year, and average bedside tenure is roughly three years. Every departure restarts an expensive, slow hiring cycle.

Costly reliance on contingent labor

Filling gaps with travel and agency staffing carries a 20–50% markup, driving labor costs up. Faster internal fills and redeployment reduce dependence on premium contingent labor.

Long hiring cycles and open-role risk

Delays in continuous, high-volume clinical hiring create patient-safety and accreditation exposure. Screening has to be fast and consistent without cutting corners on fairness or compliance.

What you would deploy

Agentic talent intelligence, applied to HCA's clinical workforce.

Screen and match clinicians at the scale HCA hires, fairly and around the clock, then keep them longer with clear internal pathways.

AI Interviewer

Structured, high-volume clinical screening that runs 24/7 and multilingually, returning scored, consistent results in hours so recruiters move faster without sacrificing fairness.

Talent Acquisition

Skills-based, licensure-aware clinical matching that surfaces qualified candidates by demonstrated capability and credentials, not keyword-matched resumes.

Talent Management

Internal mobility, redeployment and career pathing that give nurses and clinicians somewhere to grow, cutting turnover and the contingent-labor spend it drives.
