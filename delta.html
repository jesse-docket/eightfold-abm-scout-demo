Why Delta

Built for a skills-first, majority-frontline workforce.

Delta employs roughly 100,000 people, most of them in frontline operational roles, and has committed to a skills-first talent strategy. These are the pressures Eightfold is designed to relieve.

Internal mobility that leaks to external hiring

Roles get filled externally that current employees could grow into, hurting continuity and cost. A skills-based talent marketplace surfaces internal candidates by capability and trajectory before you post a req.

A skills-first architecture to maintain at scale

Delta dropped the degree requirement for about 94% of non-executive roles and mapped skills across the business. Keeping that taxonomy live and connected to real work is a continuous, data-heavy job Eightfold automates.

Frontline attrition in high-turnover roles

Operational and frontline roles churn fastest. Clear internal pathways, coaching and adjacent-skill matching give frontline employees somewhere to grow, so they build a career at Delta instead of leaving.

Digital, analytics and pilot/mechanic pipeline gaps

Data, analytics and hard-to-fill technical and pilot/mechanic pipelines need to be built proactively. Eightfold quantifies the skills you have, the gaps ahead, and the fastest internal paths to close them.

What you would deploy

Talent Intelligence, applied to Delta's skills-first strategy.

An AI-native skills engine that goes beyond a static talent hub, turning Delta's skills work into everyday mobility, development and workforce planning.

Talent Management

Internal mobility, a talent marketplace, redeployment, upskilling and succession planning, so the people you already have fill the roles the operation needs next.

Talent Intelligence

A dynamic skills model that keeps your taxonomy live against real work signals and gives leaders a defensible view of skills coverage, gaps and AI-readiness.

Talent Acquisition

Skills-based frontline and technical hiring that matches candidates on capability and adjacent skills, extending Delta's degree-optional, skills-first approach into every req.
